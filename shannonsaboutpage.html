All About Odd Art Around The World

Pre-Colombina Museo Rufino Tamayo. Made by the Incans in Chile between 1430 and 1530 AD


The Yagim sea-monster mask is among many interestingly odd arts from around the world. Many masks in Africa were possibly family crests. This mask is a symbol of healing power and magic, and closely associated with war and strength to provide protection from harm.


This Dogu statue is a Japanese sculpture from the Jomon period. It was made from limestone and quartzite composition stone.many have hypothesized that this statue was influenced by early alien contact to voodoo practices.


The Gundestrup Cauldron is a gilded silver bowl. It dates back to the 1st century BCE, and shows a clear influence from Celtic art and mythology.
